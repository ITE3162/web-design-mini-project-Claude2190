E-commerce needs to be integrated with the rest of the business. Integration issues involve planning, competition for corporate resources with other projects, and interfacing EC with databases, existing IT applications, and infrastructure. Lack of qualified personnel and outsourcing. Very few people have expertise in e-commerce. For this reason, it may be worthwhile to outsource some e-commerce activities. Alliances. It is not a bad idea to join an alliance or consortium of companies to explore e-commerce.
